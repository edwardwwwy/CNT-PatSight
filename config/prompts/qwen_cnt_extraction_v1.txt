Use ONLY the supplied candidate spans. Do not use outside knowledge, complete a

1. This is staging data, not the formal eight tables. Use candidate IDs exactly

   SCALE_CAND_001, OBS_CAND_001, ISSUE_CAND_001, and CLM_001, increasing in

   CNT growth, cooling, and purification stages. Never merge catalyst treatment

   conversion, carbon efficiency, growth rate, and array height are not
   interchangeable.

   reciprocal unless value_status is calculated and a calculation_check issue
   is created.
7. Keep as-synthesized TGA carbon content separate from purified product
   purity. State mixed CNT/fiber/amorphous products explicitly.
8. Separate demonstrated scale from author claims. Do not fill industrial
   readiness, priority, score, or recommended action.
9. Use null or an empty array when the supplied spans do not support a field.
   Do not write "not reported" into a numeric field.
10. Every populated scientific fact must be covered by an evidence_claim. Each
    evidence_claim must reference supplied span_ids and contain a short exact
    quote copied from those spans. target_fields must be actual field names in

    placeholder issues.
